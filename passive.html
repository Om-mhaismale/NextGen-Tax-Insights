1. Cryptocurrency

  • Staking: Locking up cryptocurrency in a wallet to earn rewards.
  • Yield Farming: Providing liquidity to DeFi protocols for interest or tokens.
  • Dividends from Crypto Assets: Earning dividends from certain cryptocurrencies.

2. Stocks

  • Dividends: Regular payments to shareholders from company profits.
  • REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts): Earning dividends from real estate investments.
  • Index Funds and ETFs: Investing in funds that provide dividends and diversification.

3. Gold

  • Gold ETFs: Exposure to gold price movements without physical ownership.
  • Gold Savings Schemes: Accumulating gold over time for future returns.
  • Physical Gold Rental: Renting out physical gold for income.

4. Insurance

  • Endowment Plans: Life coverage combined with savings and payouts at maturity.
  • ULIPs (Unit Linked Insurance Plans): Combining life insurance with investment options.
  • Annuities: Guaranteed income payments for a specified period or for life.

5. Property

  • Rental Income: Earning monthly rent from residential or commercial properties.
  • Real Estate Crowdfunding: Investing in real estate projects through crowdfunding platforms.
  • REITs: Investing in a diversified portfolio of properties for dividend income.
