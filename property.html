Understanding Property Investment

Property investment means buying real estate (land, houses, or apartments) to own or earn money from it. In India, property is seen as a valuable long-term investment that can increase in value and provide income.

Key Points

  • Types of Property:
    • Residential: Houses or apartments bought for personal use or rental income.
    • Commercial: Offices or shops leased to businesses.
    • Land: Land can be developed or sold when prices increase.
  • Appreciation: Property prices often increase due to area development or improvements, adding to its value over time.
  • Rental Income: Properties generate regular income when rented out, commonly with residential or commercial properties.
  • Maintenance and Costs: Owning property includes additional expenses, like property tax and repairs.
  • Capital Gains Tax: When selling, profit earned on property may be taxed, depending on how long it was held.
