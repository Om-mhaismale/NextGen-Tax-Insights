Understanding Insurance

Insurance is a way to protect yourself financially from unexpected events, like accidents, illnesses, or natural disasters. By paying a fixed amount (called a premium) to an insurance company, you get financial support if certain events occur.

Key Points

  • Types of Insurance:
    • Health Insurance: Covers medical expenses in case of illness.
    • Life Insurance: Provides financial support to your family if something happens to you.
    • Vehicle Insurance: Covers damages to your car or bike due to accidents or theft.
    • Home Insurance: Covers damage to your home from events like fires or floods.
  • Premiums: Regular payments to keep your policy active. The amount varies based on the type and level of coverage.
  • Claim: If a covered event occurs, you can make a claim to receive financial support or coverage from the insurance company.
  • Sum Assured: The maximum amount the company will pay out for a claim, especially in life insurance policies.
  • Tax Benefits: Many insurance policies in India offer tax-saving benefits, making them a smart financial choice.
